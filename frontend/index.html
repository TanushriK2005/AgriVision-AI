Yield Prediction Algorithm

Our crop yield engine evaluates historical agricultural yields using a Random Forest regression model. By feeding local region parameters, crop varieties, pesticide tonnage, and temperature, the system helps model farming productivity.

  • Region-based adjustments
  • Multi-crop support
  • Meteorological correlations

Computer Vision Diagnostics

Utilizing high-performance deep transfer learning, the disease detector classifies leaf anomalies from crop leaf images. Grounded in MobileNetV2 architecture, scans detect early blights, late blights, bacterial spots, and molds.

  • Instant leaf analysis
  • Confidence score calculation
  • Immediate treatment remedies
